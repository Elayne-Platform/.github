## What Is Elayne?

Elayne is an estate settlement app for families after loss.

When someone passes away, there is a long list of legal, financial, and administrative work that follows. Accounts need to be found. Documents need to be organized. Benefits may need to be claimed. Property, insurance, debts, and family information all need to be understood before the estate can move forward.

Most families are expected to figure this out during one of the hardest moments of their lives. Elayne gives them a place to start, a clear plan, and support as the work unfolds.

## What We Do

Elayne turns the confusion after a death into a clear estate file and a practical path forward.

Families can use Elayne to:

- Get oriented and understand what needs to happen next
- Upload key documents and organize estate information in one place
- Build an inventory of known assets, debts, accounts, and property
- Invite family members or professionals when help is needed
- Prepare for probate, attorney review, and other next steps
- Find accounts, insurance, property, benefits, and other estate information that may be easy to miss
- Map who is involved, who may need to be notified, and how the estate should move forward

Elayne is built to be simple for families and useful for the professionals who support them.

## The Elayne App

The app helps families move from "I do not know where to start" to "I know what needs to happen next."

| Step | What Elayne Helps With |
| --- | --- |
| Get oriented | Understand the estate, your role, and the first steps to take. |

## Why It Matters

Estate settlement is often slow, fragmented, and expensive. It can take hundreds of hours, involve many institutions, and leave families unsure whether they found everything or took the right next step.

Elayne brings structure to that process.

We help families:

- Save time on paperwork, calls, and follow-up
- Reduce missed accounts, benefits, assets, and deadlines
- Stay organized across documents, people, and tasks
- Work with professionals from a clearer starting point
- Protect sensitive estate and financial information
- Move forward with more confidence

## Built With Care

Elayne handles sensitive information at a sensitive time. Trust is part of the product.

The app is designed to protect estate documents, identity information, financial records, and family details with secure document handling, permissioned access, and safeguards built for sensitive estate information.
